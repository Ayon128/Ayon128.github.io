About Me

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Southeast University, Bangladesh. I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET), achieving a GPA of 3.83/4.00 and ranking 7th in my class. I am passionate about exploring real-world challenges through modern AI approaches. In the era of large-scale models, my focus lies in understanding model behavior, interpretability, and applying these insights to practical problems.
Research Interests: Multimodal Learning and Large Language Models (LLMs), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Trustworthy AI, with applications in Social Media Text Analysis, Low-resource Languages, and Healthcare.
